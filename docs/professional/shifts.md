# Shifts

Shifts are the core of your professional workflow. By creating shifts, you make yourself available for client appointments on specific resources during event hours.

---

## Creating a Shift

To create a new shift:

1. Navigate to **Create Shift** from the nav or go to **My Shifts** → **Create Shift**
2. Fill in the shift details:

| Field | Description |
|:------|:------------|
| **Event** | Select the event you're participating in |
| **Resource** | Choose an available resource for this event |
| **Start Time** | When your shift begins |
| **End Time** | When your shift ends |

3. Click **Create Shift**
4. The system validates your shift and creates it if approved

### Validation Rules

The system checks several rules before allowing a shift:

- **Event hours**: Your shift must fall within the event day's configured hours
- **Blackout periods**: You cannot create shifts during blackout times (e.g., lunch breaks)
- **Resource capacity**: The resource must have available capacity (based on quantity × professionals per unit)
- **No double-booking**: You cannot have overlapping shifts
- **Timeslot granularity**: Shifts align to the configured timeslot duration (default: 30 minutes)

{: .note }
If a resource shows as unavailable, it may be at full capacity for that time period. Try a different time or resource.

## My Shifts

The **My Shifts** page lists all your shifts:

| Column | Description |
|:-------|:------------|
| **Date/Time** | Shift start and end time |
| **Resource** | Resource name and location |
| **Capacity** | Resource capacity settings |
| **Bookings** | Number of client bookings on this shift |

You can sort and filter the list to find specific shifts.

---

## Shift Details

Click on a shift to see full details:

- Shift time range
- Resource information (name, location, capacity)
- List of assigned client bookings with client names and appointment times

---

## Cancelling a Shift

To cancel a shift:

1. Find the shift in **My Shifts**
2. Click the cancel action
3. Confirm the cancellation

{: .warning }
You **cannot cancel** a shift that has active client bookings. Contact your admin to reassign or cancel the client bookings first, then cancel the shift.

{: .note }
Cancelled shifts free up resource capacity, allowing other professionals to book that time slot.

---

## Tips for Shift Management

- **Plan ahead** — Book shifts early to secure your preferred resources and times
- **Check capacity** — If your preferred resource is full, check if other resources are available
- **Monitor bookings** — Keep an eye on your [Bookings](bookings) page to see incoming client appointments
- **Coordinate with your team** — Use the calendar view to see how your schedule fits with the overall event
