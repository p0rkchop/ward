# Professional Guide

As a **Professional**, you book shifts on resources during events to create availability, and clients book appointments on your shifts.

---

## Navigation

Your professional navigation bar provides access to:

| Page | Purpose |
|:-----|:--------|
| [Dashboard](dashboard) | Overview with upcoming shifts and recent bookings |
| [Calendar](calendar) | Visual calendar of your shifts and appointments |
| [Create Shift](shifts#creating-a-shift) | Book a new shift on a resource |
| [My Shifts](shifts) | View and manage all your shifts |
| [Bookings](bookings) | View your assigned client appointments |
| [Settings](../settings) | Display preferences (theme, timezone, etc.) |

---

## How It Works

1. An **Admin** creates an event and assigns resources to it
2. You receive the **professional password** from your admin
3. You **create shifts** by selecting an event, a resource, and a time range
4. **Clients** see your available timeslots and book appointments
5. You **view your bookings** to see which clients are assigned to your shifts

---

## Quick Start

1. **Check available events** — From the dashboard or calendar, see which events have resources available
2. **Create a shift** — Go to [Create Shift](shifts#creating-a-shift) and book yourself on a resource during event hours
3. **Monitor bookings** — Check [Bookings](bookings) regularly to see your upcoming client appointments
