# Bookings

The Bookings page shows all client appointments assigned to your shifts.

---

## Viewing Your Bookings

Navigate to **Bookings** from the professional nav to see all your assigned client appointments.

Each booking displays:

| Field | Description |
|:------|:------------|
| **Client Name** | Name of the booked client |
| **Phone** | Client's phone number |
| **Email** | Client's email (if provided) |
| **Date/Time** | Appointment start and end time |
| **Resource** | Resource name and location |
| **Status** | Booking status — Confirmed or Cancelled |

---

## How Clients Get Assigned to You

When a client books an appointment:

1. The client selects an available timeslot from the booking page
2. The system identifies all professionals with shifts covering that timeslot
3. A professional is **randomly assigned** from those with available capacity
4. The booking is created and both you and the client receive email notifications

{: .note }
Clients do not choose specific professionals. The system auto-assigns to distribute bookings evenly across available professionals.

---

## Booking Notifications

You receive an email notification when:

- A **new booking** is assigned to one of your shifts
- A booking on your shift is **cancelled** (by the client or an admin)

See [Notifications](../notifications) for the full list of email triggers.

---

## Managing Bookings

As a professional, your bookings are **read-only**. You can view booking details but cannot modify or cancel client bookings directly.

If a booking needs to be changed:
- The **client** can cancel their own booking from their appointments page
- An **admin** can reassign or cancel bookings from the event management page
