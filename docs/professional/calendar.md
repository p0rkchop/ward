# Calendar

The Calendar page provides a visual timeline of your shifts and client appointments.

---

## What You See

The calendar displays:

- **Your booked shifts** — color-coded by resource
- **Client appointments** — shown within each shift block
- **Available resource slots** — open periods where you could book additional shifts

---

## Navigating the Calendar

- Use the date navigation to move between days, weeks, or months
- Click on a shift to see its details (resource, time, bookings)
- Use the calendar to identify gaps in your schedule where you could add more shifts

---

## Color Coding

Shifts are color-coded by resource, making it easy to distinguish between different resources at a glance.

{: .note }
The calendar reflects your timezone preference from [User Settings](../settings). If times look incorrect, check your timezone setting.
