# My Appointments

The Appointments page shows all your bookings — past, present, and future.

---

## Viewing Your Appointments

Navigate to **My Appointments** from the client nav to see your full booking list.

Each appointment displays:

| Field | Description |
|:------|:------------|
| **Date/Time** | Appointment start and end time |
| **Professional** | Name of the assigned professional |
| **Resource** | Resource name and location |
| **Status** | Confirmed or Cancelled |

---

## Cancelling an Appointment

To cancel a booking:

1. Find the appointment in your list
2. Click the **Cancel** action
3. Confirm the cancellation

When you cancel:
- The timeslot becomes available for other clients
- You receive a **cancellation confirmation email**
- The assigned professional receives a notification that the booking was removed

{: .note }
Cancelled appointments remain visible in your list for reference, marked with a "Cancelled" status.

---

## Appointment Changes by Admin

An admin may occasionally:

- **Reassign your booking** to a different professional or timeslot — you'll receive an email with the updated details
- **Cancel your booking** — you'll receive a cancellation email

If your appointment is changed by an admin, check your email for the updated details.

---

## Appointment Reminders

If you have an email address on file, you'll receive a **reminder email** the morning of your appointment (sent at 8:00 AM Central Time).

See [Notifications](../notifications) for the full list of email triggers.
