# Client Guide

As a **Client**, you can browse available appointment timeslots, book appointments, and manage your bookings.

---

## Navigation

Your client navigation bar provides access to:

| Page | Purpose |
|:-----|:--------|
| [Dashboard](dashboard) | Overview with upcoming appointments |
| [Book Appointment](booking) | Browse timeslots and book new appointments |
| [My Appointments](appointments) | View and manage your existing bookings |
| [Settings](../settings) | Display preferences (theme, timezone, etc.) |

---

## How It Works

1. **Admins** create events and assign resources
2. **Professionals** book shifts on those resources, creating availability
3. You **browse available timeslots** — the system aggregates all professional availability
4. You **select a timeslot** and the system auto-assigns a professional
5. You receive a **confirmation email** with your appointment details

{: .note }
You don't need to choose a specific professional. The system automatically assigns one based on availability.

---

## Quick Start

1. **Go to Book Appointment** — Browse available dates and timeslots
2. **Select a timeslot** — Pick the date and time that works for you
3. **Confirm your booking** — You'll receive an email confirmation
4. **Check My Appointments** — View and manage your bookings anytime
